Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to provide a better user experience and analyze website traffic. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. They help improve the performance and functionality of the website and track usage.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of the website.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect data on how users interact with the website (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used for targeting ads based on user behavior.

How You Can Manage Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to disable cookies, but doing so may affect your user experience on our website.
